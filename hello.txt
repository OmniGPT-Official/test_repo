Agentic engineering is the discipline of designing, building, and operating autonomous or semi-autonomous AI agents that can perceive context, make decisions, and take actions to achieve defined goals. It combines software engineering, systems design, and applied AI to orchestrate reasoning, tool use, memory, and feedback loops in a reliable and controllable way.
